How To Create a Top 20 List:

1. Go to the Product Recommend Lists page
[Manage Recommend Lists > Product Recommend Lists]

2. Specify the Concept, [Top 20] List Type, and Product Type. Click [Create New Product Recommend List]
If the recommend list for that Concept, List Type, and Product Type exists, you will be taken to the edit page for that list.

3. Specify the Item ID for a product and click [Add].
You can use [Find Items] to search the database for Item ID.
Use the [Up] and [Down] buttons to reorder items in the list.
Repeat for all items in the Top 20 list.

4. Click [Back to [Product] Recommendation List] to Submit your changes.
Click [Submit] or [Submit All Changes] to commit the changes.
